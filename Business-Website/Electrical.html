Yes — we’re fully licensed and insured for all residential electrical work. Our team meets all state and local requirements, so you can have confidence knowing your home is in qualified, professional hands.Nevada State Contractor's Board License #12345678.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, we offer financing to make your electrical projects more manageable. From major upgrades to emergency repairs, we make it easier to get the job done without delay.

What areas do you service?

We proudly serve Las Vegas, NV and surrounding neighborhoods — excluding Henderson. If you're unsure whether your location falls within our service area, feel free to contact us and we’ll be happy to confirm.

​Do you offer emergency electrical services?

Our company offers 24/7 emergency electrical services to ensure your safety and peace of mind. Whether it’s a sudden power outage, sparking wires, or a breaker that won’t reset, our licensed electricians are ready to respond quickly and resolve the issue safely—day or night. If you suspect an immediate danger to life or property—such as a fire, explosion, or other imminent hazard—please do not wait: call 9‑1‑1 immediately to reach the fire department or police.

​Do you provide free estimates or consultations?

Our company is proud to provide professional consultations and detailed estimates for all your home electrical needs. Whether you're planning a remodel, need a repair, or want to upgrade your system, we’ll carefully assess your project and provide a clear, upfront quote. A consultation fee applies, which ensures you receive expert guidance and a thorough evaluation—so you can make informed decisions with confidence.

How do I know my electrical panel needs to be upgraded?

Frequent breaker trips, flickering lights, or the use of multiple power strips can be signs your panel is outdated. If your home is over 25 years old or you're adding major appliances, it's worth having a licensed electrician evaluate your system.
