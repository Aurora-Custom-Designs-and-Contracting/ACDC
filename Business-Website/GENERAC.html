 How it Works

Generac standby generators automatically detect power outages and restore electricity to your home within seconds—no manual startup needed. Powered by natural gas or propane, these systems are fully integrated with your electrical panel for seamless, hands-free operation.

 Power & Coverage 

Whether you need whole-home protection or just critical circuits, we offer a range of generator sizes to fit your home’s power demands. Our team will help you choose the right system based on square footage, appliance loads, and essential needs like HVAC, refrigeration, and lighting.

Automatic Power Outage Detection System

Using smart technology and an automatic transfer switch, the system continuously monitors your power and automatically restores electricity during outages, with real-time updates available through remote monitoring.

QUESTIONS?

How does a Generac home standby generator work?

A Generac standby generator automatically detects a power outage, starts within seconds, and delivers electricity directly to your home’s electrical system—no extension cords or manual startup required.

What fuel sources can the generator use?

Generac home standby generators run on either natural gas or  liquid propane (LP), giving you flexible installation options based on your property and local utility access.

Can a generator power my entire home?

Yes! Depending on the model and your home’s electrical load, we can size a system to power either your entire home or just essential circuits like HVAC, refrigerators, lights, and outlets.

How often does my generator need maintenance?

Like any engine, your Generac generator needs annual maintenance , including oil changes, filter replacement, and system checks—we offer service plans to keep yours running reliably.

What is Mobile Link™ and how does it work?

Mobile Link™ is Generac’s remote monitoring system that allows you to check your generator’s status, receive notifications, and schedule maintenance from your smartphone or computer.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes—we take care of the entire installation process, including permits, inspections, utility coordination, and final testing to ensure everything is up to code.

How long will a generator run during an outage?

As long as it has fuel, your generator can run continuously for days —natural gas provides an unlimited supply, while propane runtime depends on tank size.

Is financing available?

Yes! We offer flexible financing options to make your backup power investment more affordable, with plans that fit a range of budgets.
